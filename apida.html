Asian Pacific Islander Desi American (APIDA)

701 Night Market - Every year, we collaborate to several identity clubs where they would showcase foods and activities that represent their culture to the student body! I had the pleasure to work with a wonderful committee to organize this awesome event with +300 attendees
Asian Gala - We have collaborated in another club's, SAAB, annual event called Asian Gala where Asian cultural clubs perform to showcase their traditional performances and foods.
