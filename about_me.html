About Me

My name is Thao (pronounced as tau) and I'm currently a senior at Bucknell University majoring in computer science with a minor in film/media studies.

 

As both a programmer and cinephile, I can craft a compelling narratives on screen with the blend of my technical skills and creative mindset. I aim to create narratives that are not only visually captivating but also advocate for a more just and informed society.

 

This is reflected in my experiences as APIDA (Asian Pacific Islander Desi American) co-president and a summer research fellow. In APIDA, I fostered an Asian American community at a PWI. As a summer research fellow, I analyzed people convicted of crime's movement and security level changes in Pennsylvania's the incarceration system.
